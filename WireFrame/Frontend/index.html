LENDING & LOAN

Instead of simply holding onto cryptocurrency and hoping it will appreciate, individuals and organizations can lend up to half value of their assets and collect interest on it

Swissbit Exchange is a platform for secure and convenient P2P transfers, instead of simply holding onto crytocurrency and hoping it will appreciate, individuals and organizations can lend up to half the to-date value of their assets and collect interest on it. Annual interest rates are 8 percent and the amount is paid instantly to the lender. Meanwhile, borrowers benefit from not having to rely exclusively on traditional financial institutions. One of the first offerings of its kind, Swissbit Exchange insurance product providers peace of mind that your valuable assets are as safe as possible from theft. Inspired as a responses to the landmark breach of the Mt. Gox exchange in Tokyo, this insurance is offered through a partnership. It functions alongside other protections including proof-of-address, KYC, AML, and 2-factor authentication to fend off a wide variety of cybersecurity threats.
